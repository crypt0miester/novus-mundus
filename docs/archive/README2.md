## The Living Economy

In Novus Mundus, **Novi** is the lifeblood that fuels your ascension to power. Unlike traditional games with artificial resets, our world operates on a **continuous economy** where:

- **Time is Power**: Novi generates passively based on your tier, rewarding active and strategic players
- **Action Creates Value**: Attack other players, collect resources from your Farmers, Miners, and Bankers to accumulate cash
- **Permanent Progress**: Your rank, tier, and achievements are permanent - build your legacy that lasts
- **Strategic Burning**: Every action (hiring units, attacking, collecting resources) burns Novi, creating natural scarcity and value

## Game Rules (Simplified for Continuous Play)

1. **Entry Fee**: One-time payment of 0.1 SOL to play (anti-bot measure)
2. **New Player Protection**: 24-hour immunity shield when you start
3. **Starting Resources**: 100M Cash, 30K weapons, 10K of each unit type, 6K vehicles
4. **Free Players Start With**: 2,500 Novi to get going
5. **Safe Storage**: Solo players can protect 75% of cash in a safebox (not lootable)
6. **Novi Rules**:
   - Purchased/deposited Novi **stays in-game** and cannot be withdrawn
   - Novi won from events **CAN be withdrawn** to your wallet
   - Unused Novi remains in your account permanently (no resets!)
7. **Active Engagement**: Players inactive for 90+ days may enter "hibernation" mode (protected but no generation)
